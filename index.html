Our history

1964

Texel Boat Rental

My father starts a boat rental business on the island of Texel, Noord-Holland.

1966

Mercury Dealer

Becomes an authorised Mercury outboard dealer — the brand that defines the business.

1974

Oudeschild Watersports

Opens watersports shop in Oudeschild. Johnson, Yamaha and Honda join the portfolio.

Today

Belgium — Online

Operating from Paal-Beringen with 4000+ parts and a global customer base.

Why we exist

Outboards in
our DNA.

I grew up surrounded by 2-stroke engines, watching my father keep boats running on Texel. Today, from Belgium, I continue that same passion with an extensive collection of vintage Mercury engines and a large stock of parts.

"Classic outboards don't belong on shelves — they belong running."

We stock new, original, and quality used parts for Mercury, OMC, Yamaha, and Suzuki. Every part is clearly labelled OEM or aftermarket — no surprises.
